Key Laws of Chemical Combination

  • Law of Conservation of Mass: Matter is neither created nor destroyed.
  • Law of Constant Proportions: A chemical compound always contains the same elements in the same proportion.
  • Law of Multiple Proportions: When two elements combine in more than one ratio, the ratios are simple whole numbers.

Mole Concept

1 mole = 6.022 × 10²³ particles (Avogadro’s number). It relates mass to number of particles.
